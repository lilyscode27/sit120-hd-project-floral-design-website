Helpful Advice

Flower Care Tips

Keep your blooms fresh and beautiful for longer with these expert tips, visual guides, and resources.

Change Water Regularly

Replace the vase water every 2-3 days to prevent bacteria growth and keep stems hydrated.

Trim the Stems

Cut stems at a 45° angle before placing them in water to help them absorb nutrients more efficiently.

Avoid Direct Sunlight

Place flowers in a cool, shaded spot away from heat sources and direct sunlight to prevent wilting.

Common Flower Problems

Wilting: Usually caused by dehydration or heat exposure.

Browning Petals: Often a sign of aging blooms or bacteria in water.

Drooping Stems: May happen if stems aren’t cut at an angle or water is low.
